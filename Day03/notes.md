# 3 Terms in total

- Operators -> Symbols to carry out calculations (+-\*/)
- Operands -> Symbols that are being operated on (numbers, variables, etc.) ( x + y -> x and y are operands)
- Expression -> A combination of operators and operands (x + y, 2 \* 3, etc.)
